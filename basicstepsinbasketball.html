Basic Steps in Playing Basketball

Shooting - a two-handed shot from a distance away from the hoop in which the player remains still and shoots the ball, usually at chest level.

Dribbling - is a basic technique in which a player continuously bounces the ball off the court with one hand. You can control the ball and move it closer to the basket by dribbling.

Defending - refers to the positioning, alignments, and defensive tactics a team use to stop the other offense from putting up points.

Rebounding - a rebound, sometimes colloquially referred to as a board, is a statistic awarded to a player who retrieves the ball after a missed field goal or free throw.

Passing - is the act of throwing the ball to a teammate. One player will try to pass the ball to their teammate while the other player tries to steal the ball and take it away.
